What We Do

Model Rocketry Society provides students with continuous engagement through:

🚀

Workshops

Rocketry & Aeromodelling Workshops designed to give hands-on experience in building and launching models.

🏆

Competitions

Online and Offline Competitions that challenge students to apply their knowledge and creativity in real-world scenarios.

🎤

Expert Talks

Expert talks & guest lectures from industry veterans to inspire and guide aspiring young minds.

🌱

Outreach

Outreach and STEM awareness programs to spread the joy of science to every corner of the community.

🛠️

Hands-on Projects

Hands-on project development where students build real working models and learn engineering principles.

Programme Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

PSO1

Design and fabricate model rocket systems integrating propulsion, structures, and recovery mechanisms using engineering principles and tools.

PSO2

Apply simulation and experimental techniques (such as OpenRocket, ANSYS, MATLAB, and wind tunnel testing) to analyse and optimise rocket performance.

PSO3

Conduct experimental launches and interpret flight data for research, documentation, and technical reporting in aerospace applications.
