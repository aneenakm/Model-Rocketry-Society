Model Rocketry Society

The Model Rocketry Society is a student-driven community passionate about rockets, Space, and Aerospace Engineering. We bring together curious minds who love to Design, Build, and Launch Rockets while learning real Engineering skills in a fun and Practical way.

Our society conducts Workshops, Hands-on projects, Launch Events, and Technical discussions to help students Explore Aerodynamics, Propulsion, Structures, Avionics, and Space Science beyond textbooks. We believe in learning by doing, Teamwork, Creativity, and safe rocketry practices.

Whether you are a beginner or an Aspiring Aerospace Engineer, the Model Rocketry Society provides a platform to Explore your Ideas, build Innovative projects, and prepare for Future Careers in Space and Advanced Technology.

Our Vision

We envision becoming a premier student rocketry society dedicated to advancing aerospace engineering education, innovation, and research. The society aims to bridge academic knowledge with practical engineering through structured rocketry programs and experimentation. By fostering technical excellence and scientific integrity, we strive to develop future leaders in aerospace and space technology.

Our Mission

  • To provide structured training and hands-on experience in model and experimental rocketry.
  • To promote research, innovation, and multidisciplinary collaboration in aerospace engineering.
  • To organize technical workshops, launch campaigns, and competitions for skill development.
  • To uphold the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and ethical engineering practices.

Our Inspiration

DR. G. MADHAVAN NAIR

Ex ISRO Chairman Distinguished Space Scientist

Dr. G. Madhavan Nair is a renowned Indian space scientist and former Chairman of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), with several decades of experience in leading space technology and space missions for the nation. Dr. Nair has been instrumental in promoting India's space development efforts in areas such as satellite launch vehicles, remote sensing, and space exploration missions. He is currently the principal head and mentor of the Model Rocketry Society, leading the society's vision and strategy.

DR. M. R. K. MENON

CEO - Global Aerosports Secretary - Model Rocketry Society

Dr. (Hon.) M. R. K. Menon is a pioneer in Aeromodelling, Model Rocketry, Drone Technology, Robotics, and Space Technology with 20+ years of global experience in STEM education. He has trained over 100,000 students and 200+ teachers across multiple countries and is the Secretary of the Model Rocketry Society and Adviser to leading aerospace organizations.
